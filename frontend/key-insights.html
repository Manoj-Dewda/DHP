Actionable Recommendations

Practical Tips

Resume Building
  • Highlight projects related to in-demand domains like Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Focus on demonstrating technical skills that match job requirements
  • Include relevant certifications from recognized platforms
  • Quantify your achievements wherever possible
  • Customize your resume for each application to highlight relevant skills
Interview Preparation
  • Research target companies and understand their tech stack
  • Practice technical questions related to your domain
  • Prepare domain-specific real-world problem solutions
  • Review fundamentals as they are frequently tested
  • Be ready to discuss projects from your resume in detail
Salary Negotiation
  • Research average internship salaries for your domain and location
  • Highlight specialized skills that command higher compensation
  • Consider remote opportunities which may offer competitive pay
  • Discuss learning opportunities along with compensation
  • Be flexible but know your minimum acceptable offer
Skill Development
  • Focus on learning skills in high-demand domains
  • Build a portfolio of projects demonstrating your capabilities
  • Participate in open source projects related to your field
  • Take online courses to learn trending technologies
  • Join communities and networks in your domain of interest
